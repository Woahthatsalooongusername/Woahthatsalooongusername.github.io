Scrambled Eggs with Cheese

This is a super simple recipe you can make in a short amount of time. My family and i commonly make this for breakfast.

Time: 5-7 minutes
Servings: 1-2

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp shredded cheese
  1. Crack eggs onto a bowl and throw a pinch of salt and pepper in
  2. Heat butter in a pan over a medium heat
  3. Pour in the eggs and stir gently with a spatula
  4. When the eggs start to set, sprinkle in some cheese
  5. Stir until the eggs are creamy and fully cooked
  6. Enjoy!
