Garlic Butter Pasta

Time: 15-20 minutes
Servings: 2

Ingredients:

  • 200g (7 oz) Spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 cups water or broth
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: grated Parmesan or Parsley
  1. In a pot, melt butter over a medium heat
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds
  3. Add Spaghetti and water/broth. Bring to a boil
  4. Cook uncovered, stir occasionally, untill pasta absorbs most of the liquid (10-12 minutes)
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Add grated Parmesan or Parsley if you want
  6. Enjoy!
